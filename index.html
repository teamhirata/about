About us

Language precision at the core of AI

Team Hirata is a professional AI language training team based in Indonesia. We work with international AI product teams to make sure their models produce accurate, natural, and contextually appropriate responses in Indonesian and English.

We bring together linguists, journalists, translators, editors, and content strategists. People who have spent careers thinking carefully about language. Every project goes through structured briefing, calibration, and a quality review layer before results are delivered.

Our work spans writing AI responses, side-by-side evaluation, defect annotation, and classification tasks, all grounded in a genuine understanding of how language works rather than how it merely looks on the surface.

01

Data preparation

We gather, organise, and label the inputs your AI needs to learn from (text, responses, queries) making sure the data is accurate, well-structured, and representative of real-world use.

02

Output evaluation

We go through your AI's responses carefully, assessing accuracy, natural language quality, and real-world appropriateness in Indonesian and English. Everything is documented with clear reasoning so your technical team knows exactly what to address.

03

Human-in-the-loop feedback

We flag errors, inconsistencies, and edge cases that automated checks miss, and provide structured feedback that guides the model toward better behaviour over time. Human judgment is at the centre of this, not just pattern-matching against a rubric.

04

Continuous refinement

We track performance over time and update our approach as your model evolves or requirements shift, staying in close contact with your product and engineering teams throughout.

What we do

Our services

We cover the full range of tasks that AI language training requires, from preparing datasets to evaluating outputs and refining model behaviour over time.

Data labelling and organisation

Labelling text, queries, and responses so your AI can recognise patterns and make reliable decisions. Covers cleaning, structuring, and preparing datasets for training.

Output evaluation

Reviewing AI-generated results against real-world expectations. We assess accuracy, relevance, and natural language quality with human judgment, not just rubric-checking.

Human-in-the-loop training

Intervening when the AI makes mistakes, guiding model behaviour with direct human feedback, and helping the system learn from edge cases it would not handle well on its own.

Conversational AI training

Reviewing AI interactions, correcting errors in tone and phrasing, and refining responses to make them sound natural and appropriate for the target audience.

Generative AI training

Evaluating AI-generated text and providing structured feedback to guide the model toward more accurate, coherent, and contextually appropriate outputs.

Domain specialist training

Bringing Indonesian and English language expertise to AI that needs to perform in specific domains: finance, e-commerce, news, entertainment, and general-purpose assistants.
